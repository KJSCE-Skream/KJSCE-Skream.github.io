Contigency Rules

Winners, Runners-up & Semi-finalists will be awarded points as per the following :

Team Events
  • The winning team will be awarded 15 points
  • The runners-up team will be awarded 9 points
  • Semi-finalists will be awarded 5 points
Individual Events
  • The winning team will be awarded 10 points
  • The runners-up team will be awarded 6 points
  • Semifinalists will be awarded 3 points
  • Points awarded for participation: 2 for individuals, 5 for teams
  • Points will be awarded for online contest. For more details, visit our Facebook page : KJSCE Skream

General Rules

  • The last date for filling the registration form is 25th December. Any registration entered beyond the last date will not be considered.
  • Players are required to carry the bonafide and ID Cards issued by the college for all matches. All players will have to sign an undertaking before the match. Players will not be allowed to play without fulfilling the above formalities
  • The umpires’/referees’ decision is final.
  • Players/teams should report at the venue 30 minutes prior to the match time. If the player/team exceeds this time limit, the player/team will have to forfeit the match.
  • The College and the Skream Organising Committee will not be held responsible for any injury or mishaps sustained during the course of the competition.
  • Misconduct on/off the field will be severely dealt with by the OC
  • Any disputes should be brought to the notice of the event head or any member of the Skream OC.
  • The Skream OC reserves the right to debar any player/team for failing to meet the required standards of conduct.
  • Prize money is subject to change depending on the number of entries.
NOTE: Football will not be considered in the contingency
